Building, not a floor plan

Every level has a different pace.

01 · Basement

Quiet work

Away from the street noise, the basement is fitted out for people who need a table, a socket, and a few uninterrupted hours. Softer lighting, fewer conversations, more focus.

02 · Ground Floor

Deck & greenery

Step onto the wooden outdoor deck, wrapped in plants and open sunlight. This is Habidade's main room — lush, unhurried, built for long breakfasts and longer conversations.

03 · Upper Floor

Live events

The top floor turns into a stage after dark — live sets, open mics, and evenings that run later than the coffee menu. Same building, different energy.
